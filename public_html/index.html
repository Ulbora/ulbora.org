Ulbora.org

Ulbora.org is the home of the Ulbora Software Foundation.

The Ulbora Software Foundation is an organization and repository devoted to open-source full-stack JavaScript development.

Our Mission
Our mission is to facilitate the development of applications built with full-stack JavaScript. We have many projects in the works and many projects planned. Check out UlboraCMS, our MEAN stack CMS and blog application. MEAN stands for MongoDB, ExpressJS, AngularJS and Node.js.
